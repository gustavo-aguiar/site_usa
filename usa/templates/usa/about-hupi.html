About Hupi

Founded in 2016, Hupi is starting its operations in the area of 3D printing of houses, focused on rapid and quality development, aimed at the low-income public;

We believe that people with a low income also have the right to a beautiful and comfortable home;

We have a wide experience in real estate development, adding up 573 000 m² built and more than 4 320 apartments delivered in the last 10 years in Brazil and in Paraguay.

Purpose: Deliver decent, beautiful and affordable houses.

Mission: To be the best real estate developer in Latin América in 10 years.
