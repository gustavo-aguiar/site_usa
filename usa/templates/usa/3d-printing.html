3D Printing differentials

Fast: Construction of houses in 24 hours or less;

Efficiency: With the ability to fill walls quickly and accurately, 3D printing can save resources by maximizing the use of team for more practical processes, such as using forklifts to build the roof and manually placing windows;

Sustainable: Developments in 3D-printed residences show that low-cost housing can be affordable, comfortable end even elegant. The possibilities are real for millions of peole around the world who struggle with basic housing;

Social: Potential to solve the housing crisis with low-income housing, combat natural disasters with immediate shelters end improve the industry construction with more efficient workplaces.
